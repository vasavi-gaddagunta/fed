ABOUT COFFEE :

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain flowering plants in the Coffea genus. From the coffee fruit,
the seeds are separated to produce a stable, raw product: unroasted green coffee. The seeds are then roasted, a process which transforms
them into a consumable product: roasted It is usually served hot, although chilled or iced coffee is common.
Sugar, sugar substitutes, milk or cream are often used to lessen
the bitter taste or enhance the flavor. It may be served with coffee cake or
another sweet dessert, like doughnuts.
A commercial establishment that sells prepared coffee beverages is known as a coffeehouse or coffee shop
(not to be confused with Dutch coffeeshops selling cannabis).
